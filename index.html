About Me

Ah, the eternal problem of attempting to succinctly describe yourself. There`s a few strategies you can take. You can give some facts about yourself, like your age, your political beliefs and/or your religion/life-philosophy. You could list some of the things your interested in, like movies, comparative religions, political philosophy and whatever else hold my, fuck, their interest. Some people like to list a few things they dislike, usually with a silly item mixed in. Something to the effect of I dislike war crimes, food poisoning and butter on toast in reverse order.. Or you could do all of the above, but refer to them in a meta way to seem wittily self-aware. Personally, I think the last option is a bit cheesy, but I`m not lactose-intolerant so thats not a huge concern for me.

Shit I Recommend

Movies

  • Housefull 4 | I have never laughed more at something
  • Children of Men | The first movie that was beautiful to me
  • The Perks of Being a Wallflower | Out of any character in fiction, I resonate most with the main character of this movie
  • The Martian | I love that there are no villains
  • Before Sunrise & Before Sunset | Both of these film build such a quietly beautiful love story

Books

  • The Conquest of Bread | It asked a question, `What if we are already rich enough to feed everyone?`, that I couldnt answer
  • The Unique and Its Property | The book that saved my life
  • The Stranger | The other book that saved my life
  • Dune | Its the last fiction book I finished
  • Tao Te Ching | There is no way I can't recommend it

Music

  • Sleeping at Last | Saturn is my favorite song
  • Daft Punk | The Tron Legacy OST and Lose Yourself to Dance are my favorites
  • Isak Danielson | I love the emotion in all of his songs

Anime
