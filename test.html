About Me

Ah, the eternal problem of attempting to succinctly describe yourself. There's a few strategies you can take. You can give some facts about yourself, like your age, your political beliefs and/or your religion/life-philosophy. You could list some of the things your interested in, like movies, comparative religions, political philosophy and whatever else hold my, fuck, their interest. Some people like to list a few things they dislike, usually with a silly item mixed in. Something to the effect of 'I dislike war crimes, food poisoning and butter on toast in reverse order.'. Or you could do all of the above, but refer to them in a meta way to seem wittily self-aware. Personally, I think the last option is a bit cheesy, but I'm not lactose-intolerant so that's not a huge concern for me.
